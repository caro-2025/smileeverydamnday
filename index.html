About

I believe the quiet moments are where life truly happens - the ones we so often overlook.

I'm Carola Maria, a visual storyteller with a love for fleeting details and the beauty hidden in the everyday.

Through short films, spoken word poetry and documentaries, I hope to open up new perspectives on the world, reminding us that even the ordinary is a reason to smile.

My work invites you to pause, find meaning in unexpected places, and discover the quiet magic that's always there when we choose to notice.
